# Information Security Policy

**Acme Corp** is dedicated to safeguarding its information assets, ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data critical to our operations and our clients. This Information Security Policy establishes the framework for protecting our digital and physical information resources from unauthorized access, use, disclosure, disruption, modification, or destruction.

## Objectives:

The primary objectives of this Information Security Policy are:

*   To protect sensitive company and client data from unauthorized access and breaches.
*   To maintain the operational integrity and availability of information systems.
*   To comply with all relevant legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations in India.
*   To foster a security-aware culture among all employees and contractors.
*   To ensure business continuity by minimizing the impact of security incidents.

## Scope:

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, temporary staff, and third-party vendors who access, process, or store information assets belonging to or managed by Acme Corp. This includes all company-owned or managed devices, networks, and facilities.

## Policy Guidelines:

1.  **Asset Management:**
    1.1. Maintain an accurate inventory of all information assets, including hardware, software, and data.
    1.2. Classify information assets based on their sensitivity and criticality.
    1.3. Assign ownership and responsibility for the management and protection of each information asset.

2.  **Access Control:**
    2.1. Implement a principle of least privilege, granting access only to information and systems necessary for job functions.
    2.2. Establish robust user authentication mechanisms, including strong password policies and multi-factor authentication where appropriate.
    2.3. Regularly review and revoke access rights upon changes in employment status or job responsibilities.

3.  **Data Protection and Encryption:**
    3.1. Implement measures to protect data at rest and in transit, including encryption for sensitive information.
    3.2. Define procedures for secure data handling, storage, and disposal.
    3.3. Ensure compliance with Indian data protection laws, such as the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

4.  **Network and System Security:**
    4.1. Deploy and maintain firewalls, intrusion detection/prevention systems, and other network security controls.
    4.2. Implement regular vulnerability scanning and penetration testing to identify and address security weaknesses.
    4.3. Ensure all systems are kept up-to-date with the latest security patches and updates.

5.  **Incident Response:**
    5.1. Establish a dedicated Incident Response Team to manage and mitigate security breaches.
    5.2. Develop and document a clear incident response plan, including reporting, containment, eradication, and recovery steps.
    5.3. Conduct post-incident reviews to identify lessons learned and improve security measures.

6.  **Physical Security:**
    6.1. Implement controls to protect physical access to company facilities, data centers, and sensitive equipment.
    6.2. Secure workstations and mobile devices when unattended.
    6.3. Manage visitor access to company premises.

7.  **Security Awareness and Training:**
    7.1. Provide mandatory security awareness training to all new employees and regular refresher training for all personnel.
    7.2. Educate employees on identifying and reporting security threats, such as phishing and social engineering attempts.
    7.3. Communicate policy updates and emerging security risks effectively.

8.  **Third-Party Risk Management:**
    8.1. Conduct due diligence on third-party vendors who will have access to Acme Corp's information assets.
    8.2. Ensure contractual agreements include appropriate security clauses and compliance requirements.
    8.3. Periodically review the security practices of critical third-party vendors.

## Responsibilities:

*   **Chief Information Security Officer (CISO):** Overall responsibility for the development, implementation, and enforcement of this policy.
*   **IT Department:** Responsible for the technical implementation and maintenance of security controls.
*   **Human Resources:** Responsible for ensuring security awareness training is provided and for enforcing policy compliance related to employee conduct.
*   **All Employees and Contractors:** Responsible for adhering to the guidelines outlined in this policy and reporting any suspected security incidents.

## Approval and Review:

This Information Security Policy is approved by the Acme Corp Executive Leadership Team. It will be reviewed and updated at least annually, or more frequently as necessitated by changes in technology, threats, or regulatory requirements.

## Distribution:

This policy will be distributed to all employees and contractors via the company intranet and will be included in new employee onboarding materials. It will also be made available to relevant third-party vendors upon request.
