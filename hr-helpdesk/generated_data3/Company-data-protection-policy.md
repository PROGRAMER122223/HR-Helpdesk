# Acme Corp Information Security Policy

## Policy Overview and Objective

Acme Corp is dedicated to safeguarding the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of all information assets. This policy outlines our commitment to protecting sensitive data belonging to our employees, clients, partners, and other stakeholders. We aim to ensure that all data is collected, stored, processed, and disposed of ethically, transparently, and in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, particularly those in India.

## Scope of Application

This policy applies to all individuals who interact with Acme Corp's information systems and data. This includes:

*   All full-time, part-time, and contract employees of Acme Corp and its affiliates.
*   Third-party vendors, consultants, contractors, and service providers who access or process Acme Corp data.
*   Job candidates during the recruitment process.
*   Clients, partners, and any other external parties who share information with Acme Corp.

## Policy Components

Acme Corp recognizes the critical importance of data and information. This encompasses all forms of data, whether digital or physical, that can identify an individual or organization, including but not limited to: personal identification details, financial records, intellectual property, client lists, and proprietary business information.

All data collected and processed by Acme Corp will adhere to the following principles:

### Data Handling Principles:

*   **Lawful and Fair Collection:** Data will be collected only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and will not be processed in a manner incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Accuracy and Timeliness:** Data will be kept accurate and up-to-date. Inaccurate data will be rectified or erased without delay.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Data will be collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a manner that is incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Data Minimization:** Only data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed will be collected.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Data will be kept in a form that permits identification of data subjects for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data are processed.
*   **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Data will be processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage, using appropriate technical or organizational measures.

### Data Usage Restrictions:

*   Data will not be shared with unauthorized internal or external parties.
*   Data will not be retained beyond the period required for its intended purpose or as mandated by law.
*   Data will not be transferred to countries or international organizations that do not ensure an adequate level of protection for personal data, unless specific safeguards are in place.
*   Data will not be disclosed to third parties without explicit consent from the data owner, except where legally required (e.g., by law enforcement agencies with valid legal orders).

### Individual Rights and Obligations:

Acme Corp acknowledges and respects the rights of individuals concerning their data. We are committed to:

*   Informing individuals about the types of data collected and the purposes of processing.
*   Providing clarity on who has access to their data.
*   Establishing robust procedures for managing data breaches, including notification to affected individuals and relevant authorities.
*   Facilitating individuals' rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, or object to the processing of their data.

## Security Measures and Practices

To uphold the principles outlined in this policy, Acme Corp will implement and maintain comprehensive security measures, including but not limited to:

*   **Access Control:** Implementing strict access controls and authorization mechanisms to limit access to sensitive data based on the principle of least privilege.
*   **Data Encryption:** Employing encryption for data both in transit and at rest where appropriate.
*   **Secure Network Infrastructure:** Maintaining secure network infrastructure to protect against cyber threats and unauthorized access.
*   **Regular Audits and Monitoring:** Conducting regular security audits and monitoring of systems and data access.
*   **Employee Training:** Providing mandatory and ongoing training to all employees on data protection, privacy best practices, and security awareness.
*   **Incident Response Plan:** Developing and regularly testing a comprehensive incident response plan for data breaches.
*   **Physical Security:** Implementing physical security measures for data storage facilities and equipment.
*   **Data Disposal:** Establishing secure procedures for the permanent disposal of data when it is no longer required.
*   **Contractual Safeguards:** Including data protection clauses in all contracts with third parties who handle Acme Corp data.

## Policy Enforcement and Disciplinary Actions

Adherence to this Information Security Policy is mandatory for all individuals covered under its scope. Any violation of this policy will be taken seriously and may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract, and may also lead to legal proceedings.

## Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed at least annually, or more frequently as required by changes in legislation, technology, or business operations, to ensure its continued effectiveness and compliance.
