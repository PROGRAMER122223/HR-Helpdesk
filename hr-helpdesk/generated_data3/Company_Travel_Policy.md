# Acme Corp Business Travel Policy

## 1. Objective:

This policy establishes the framework for all business-related travel undertaken by Acme Corp employees. Its purpose is to ensure the safety and well-being of our personnel while managing travel expenses efficiently and responsibly.

## 2. Approval Requirements:

All business travel necessitates prior authorization from the employee's direct manager or department head. This approval signifies confirmation of the travel's necessity, alignment with budgetary constraints, and adherence to this policy.

## 3. Booking Procedures:

a. To leverage cost savings, employees are strongly encouraged to book travel arrangements well in advance of the departure date.

b. All flight, accommodation, and ground transportation bookings must be processed exclusively through Acme Corp's designated online travel portal or our contracted travel management partner.

## 4. Transportation Guidelines:

a. For air travel, the standard class of service is economy. For other modes of transport, the standard class is expected.

b. The use of rental vehicles should be judicious. Employees are expected to evaluate alternative transportation methods, such as public transit or ride-sharing services, where feasible and cost-effective.

## 5. Accommodation Standards:

a. Employees are required to select accommodation from Acme Corp's list of preferred hotels or approved lodging providers.

b. Accommodation choices should be prudent and remain within the per diem allowances established for the specific travel destination.

## 6. Expense Reimbursement:

a. Acme Corp will reimburse employees for expenses that are reasonable, necessary, and directly related to approved business travel.

b. Per diem rates, covering meals and incidental expenses, will be provided based on the designated travel location and will align with the company's established reimbursement schedule.

## 7. Expense Submission:

a. All business travel expenses must be meticulously documented and submitted for reimbursement promptly upon completion of the trip.

b. Original receipts, flight e-tickets, and any other pertinent documentation must accompany all expense reports.

## 8. Travel Protection:

a. Acme Corp provides comprehensive travel insurance for employees during official business trips.

b. Employees are advised to review the policy's coverage details and to report any travel-related incidents immediately to the designated contact.

## 9. Safety and Security Protocols:

a. Employee safety is paramount. All personnel must comply with local laws, customs, and security advisories in their travel destinations.

b. In the event of an emergency, employees must contact their designated emergency point of contact at Acme Corp and follow established emergency response procedures.

## 10. Policy Adherence:

a. Strict compliance with all provisions of this Business Travel Policy is mandatory for all employees.

b. Failure to adhere to this policy may result in the denial of expense reimbursement and may lead to disciplinary action.

## 11. Policy Review:

This Business Travel Policy will undergo periodic reviews and updates to ensure its continued relevance and effectiveness. Employees will be informed of any modifications.

---

By engaging in business travel, employees signify their understanding and acceptance of the terms outlined in this Acme Corp Business Travel Policy.
