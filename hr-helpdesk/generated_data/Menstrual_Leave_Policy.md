## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

This Employee Handbook serves as a comprehensive guide to the policies, procedures, and expectations at Acme Corp. It is designed to ensure a fair, consistent, and productive work environment for all our employees. Please familiarize yourself with its contents, as adherence to these guidelines is a condition of employment.

---

### **1. Introduction**

**1.1. Welcome to Acme Corp**
Acme Corp is a leading Information Technology company dedicated to innovation, excellence, and fostering a collaborative work environment. We value our employees and are committed to providing a supportive and rewarding workplace.

**1.2. Purpose of the Handbook**
This Employee Handbook outlines Acme Corp's policies and procedures. It is intended to provide clear guidance on expectations regarding conduct, performance, benefits, and other employment-related matters. This handbook is not an employment contract and does not guarantee employment for any specific duration.

**1.3. Scope**
This handbook applies to all employees of Acme Corp, regardless of employment status (full-time, part-time, temporary) or position, unless otherwise specified.

**1.4. Policy Amendments**
Acme Corp reserves the right to amend, modify, or terminate any policy or procedure outlined in this handbook at any time, with or without notice, as deemed necessary by the company. Employees will be notified of any significant changes.

---

### **2. Employment Basics**

**2.1. Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO)**
Acme Corp is an Equal Employment Opportunity employer. We are committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all individuals without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, marital status, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by applicable Indian law. This policy applies to all terms and conditions of employment, including recruitment, hiring, placement, promotion, termination, layoff, recall, transfer, leaves of absence, compensation, and training.

**2.2. Employment At-Will**
Employment with Acme Corp is voluntary and may be terminated by either the employee or the company at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice, subject to applicable laws.

**2.3. Employee Classification**
Employees will be classified as either "Exempt" or "Non-Exempt" based on their job duties, responsibilities, and salary, in accordance with the Indian Labour Laws. This classification determines eligibility for overtime pay.

**2.4. Recruitment and Selection**
Acme Corp is committed to a fair and transparent recruitment and selection process. We aim to attract and hire qualified candidates based on merit, skills, and experience, ensuring a discrimination-free hiring process. All hiring decisions will be made in compliance with EEO principles.

**2.5. Background Checks**
As part of our commitment to maintaining a safe and reliable workforce, Acme Corp may conduct background checks on candidates for certain positions. This may include verification of employment history, education, and criminal records, conducted in compliance with applicable Indian laws.

**2.6. Employee Referral Program**
Acme Corp encourages employees to refer qualified candidates for open positions. A referral bonus may be provided for successful hires, as outlined in the detailed Employee Referral Program Policy.

**2.7. Internal Hiring Process**
We prioritize internal mobility and encourage employees to apply for open positions within the company. A clear process is in place to ensure fair consideration for internal candidates.

**2.8. Employee Rehire Policy**
Former employees may be considered for rehire based on their previous employment record and the needs of the company. Rehire decisions will be made on a case-by-case basis.

### **3. Workplace Conduct and Ethics**

**3.1. Code of Conduct**
All employees are expected to adhere to Acme Corp's Code of Conduct, which emphasizes integrity, honesty, professionalism, and respect in all workplace interactions. This includes complying with laws, protecting company assets, and maintaining a cordial atmosphere.

**3.2. Professional Code of Ethics**
Our professional code of ethics guides behavior in business contexts, ensuring legality and a healthy workplace. It is based on principles of respect, integrity, honesty, justice, lawfulness, competence, and teamwork.

**3.3. Anti-Discrimination Policy**
Acme Corp strictly prohibits discrimination and harassment based on protected characteristics such as age, religion, ethnicity, nationality, disability, gender identity, sexual orientation, etc. We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment for everyone.

**3.4. Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH)**
Acme Corp is committed to providing a safe and respectful workplace free from sexual harassment. We have established an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) to address complaints in accordance with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013. All employees are expected to familiarize themselves with and adhere to this policy.

**3.5. Workplace Violence Policy**
Acme Corp is committed to maintaining a workplace free from violence, threats, and aggressive behavior. Any form of workplace violence is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

**3.6. Workplace Gossip Policy**
Spreading rumors or engaging in gossip that harms colleagues or the workplace environment is unacceptable. Employees are encouraged to communicate directly and professionally.

**3.7. Conflict of Interest Policy**
Employees must avoid situations where their personal interests conflict with the interests of Acme Corp. Any potential or actual conflicts of interest must be disclosed to management.

**3.8. Nepotism Policy**
Acme Corp prohibits nepotism, which is the practice of favoritism towards relatives or close personal relationships in employment decisions. Hiring, promotion, and supervisory decisions will be based on merit and qualifications.

**3.9. Workplace Visitor Policy**
All visitors to Acme Corp premises must register and comply with company security and conduct guidelines. Visitors may be required to be escorted in certain areas.

**3.10. Pets in the Workplace Policy**
Acme Corp may permit employees to bring well-behaved pets to the workplace under specific conditions and with prior approval from HR. Pet owners are responsible for their pet's behavior and well-being.

**3.11. Drug and Alcohol Policy**
Acme Corp is committed to maintaining a drug-free and alcohol-free workplace. The use, possession, or distribution of illegal drugs is strictly prohibited. Alcohol consumption is regulated, and employees must not be under the influence of alcohol or drugs while at work.

**3.12. Whistleblower Policy**
Acme Corp encourages employees to report any suspected unethical, illegal, or improper conduct without fear of retaliation. The Whistleblower Policy outlines the procedures for reporting and the protections available to whistleblowers.

**3.13. Anti-Bribery and Corruption Policy**
Acme Corp strictly prohibits bribery and corruption in all business dealings. Employees must not offer, give, receive, or solicit bribes or engage in any corrupt practices.

### **4. Compensation and Benefits**

**4.1. Payroll and Salary Advances**
Acme Corp provides regular payroll processing. Salary advances may be considered in exceptional circumstances, subject to policy guidelines and approval.

**4.2. Employee Benefits and Perks**
Acme Corp offers a comprehensive benefits package, which may include health insurance, provident fund, gratuity, and other statutory benefits as per Indian law. Details of eligibility and coverage will be provided by the HR department.

**4.3. Gratuity Policy**
Gratuity is paid to eligible employees upon retirement, resignation, permanent disablement, or death, in accordance with the Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972.

**4.4. Employee Incentive and Bonus Policy**
Acme Corp may offer incentive and bonus programs to reward employees for outstanding performance and contributions to the company's success. Specific program details will be communicated separately.

**4.5. Employee Discount Policy**
Details regarding employee discounts on company products or services will be communicated by HR.

**4.6. Employee Housing Assistance Policy**
Acme Corp may provide relocation assistance or temporary housing support in specific circumstances, as outlined in the Relocation Policy.

**4.7. Employee Assistance Program (EAP)**
Acme Corp provides an Employee Assistance Program (EAP) to offer confidential counseling and support services for employees facing personal or work-related challenges.

**4.8. Long-Term Disability (LTD) Policy**
Information regarding long-term disability benefits will be provided to eligible employees.

**4.9. Short-Term Disability (STD) Policy**
Acme Corp provides short-term disability benefits to eligible employees unable to perform their duties due to a non-work-related illness or injury.

**4.10. Severance Pay Policy**
In the event of a layoff or involuntary termination for reasons not related to employee conduct, eligible employees may receive severance pay as per company policy and applicable laws.

**5.3. Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy**
Employees accrue Paid Time Off (PTO) for vacation, personal needs, and sick leave. The accrual rates and usage guidelines are detailed in the PTO policy.
