# Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy

## Policy on Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH)

**1. Policy Statement:**

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a safe, inclusive, and respectful work environment for all employees. This Policy on Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) is established to prevent, prohibit, and address incidents of sexual harassment within the workplace, in accordance with The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013.

**2. Purpose:**

The purpose of this policy is to:
*   Define and prohibit sexual harassment.
*   Establish a clear and accessible reporting mechanism for incidents of sexual harassment.
*   Provide a framework for prompt, fair, and impartial investigations and resolution of complaints.
*   Promote a culture of respect, dignity, and equality in the workplace.
*   Ensure compliance with all applicable laws and regulations pertaining to sexual harassment.

**3. Scope:**

This policy applies to all employees of Acme Corp, including full-time, part-time, temporary, contractual staff, interns, trainees, and any other individuals engaged with Acme Corp in a professional capacity, regardless of their position or tenure. This policy also extends to interactions with clients, vendors, and other third parties.

**4. Definitions:**

*   **Sexual Harassment:** Any unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature, whether verbal, non-verbal, written, or physical, that has the purpose or effect of violating an individual's dignity or creating an intimidating, hostile, degrading, humiliating, or offensive work environment. This includes, but is not limited to:
    *   Unwelcome sexual advances.
    *   Requests for sexual favors.
    *   Making sexually colored remarks or comments.
    *   Showing pornography.
    *   Any other unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature, including unwelcome gestures, jokes, or comments.
    *   Physical contact or advances of a sexual nature.
    *   Quid pro quo harassment (i.e., demanding or conditioning employment benefits on sexual favors).
*   **Complainant:** Any person who alleges an incident of sexual harassment.
*   **Respondent:** Any person against whom a complaint of sexual harassment is made.
*   **Internal Complaints Committee (ICC):** A committee constituted by Acme Corp to inquire into complaints of sexual harassment, as mandated by the POSH Act, 2013.
*   **Workplace:** Includes any place visited by the employee arising out of or during the course of employment, including transportation provided by the employer for the purpose of reaching such place.

**5. Prohibited Conduct:**

The following behaviors are strictly prohibited and may constitute sexual harassment:
*   Unwelcome physical contact or advances.
*   Demand or request for sexual favors.
*   Making sexually colored remarks or comments.
*   Showing pornography.
*   Any unwelcome messages through telephone, email, or internet of a sexual nature.
*   Any other unwelcome conduct of a sexual nature that creates a hostile work environment.

**6. Internal Complaints Committee (ICC):**

Acme Corp shall constitute an Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) in accordance with the POSH Act, 2013. The ICC will be responsible for:
*   Receiving and addressing complaints of sexual harassment.
*   Conducting inquiries into complaints.
*   Recommending appropriate action to the management.
*   Promoting a gender-sensitive work environment.

The composition and functioning of the ICC will adhere to the guidelines stipulated in the POSH Act, 2013. The ICC members will serve for a term of three years.

**7. Reporting Procedure:**

Any employee who believes they have been subjected to sexual harassment or has witnessed an incident of sexual harassment is encouraged to report the matter immediately. Reports can be made:
*   Directly to any member of the ICC.
*   To the Human Resources Department.
*   To the employee's immediate supervisor or manager.

Reports can be made orally or in writing. Oral complaints will be documented in writing by the person receiving the complaint and authenticated by the complainant.

**8. Investigation Process:**

Upon receipt of a complaint, the ICC will initiate an inquiry process in accordance with the POSH Act, 2013. This process will include:
*   Providing a copy of the complaint to the respondent.
*   Providing an opportunity to the respondent to submit a written response.
*   Examining witnesses and evidence.
*   Ensuring confidentiality throughout the investigation.
*   Completing the inquiry within a stipulated timeframe (typically 90 days).

**9. Confidentiality:**

All parties involved in the complaint process, including the complainant, respondent, witnesses, and ICC members, are expected to maintain strict confidentiality regarding the complaint, investigation, and proceedings. Information will only be disclosed on a need-to-know basis and in accordance with legal requirements.

**10. Non-Retaliation:**

Acme Corp strictly prohibits any form of retaliation, discrimination, or adverse action against any employee who, in good faith, reports an incident of sexual harassment or participates in an investigation. Any such act of retaliation will be subject to disciplinary action.

**11. Disciplinary Action:**

Any employee found to have engaged in sexual harassment will be subject to disciplinary action, which may include, but is not limited to:
*   Warning (verbal or written).
*   Transfer.
*   Demotion.
*   Suspension.
*   Termination of employment.
*   Other appropriate measures as determined by the management based on the findings of the ICC and the severity of the offense.

**12. Training and Awareness:**

Acme Corp will conduct regular training sessions and awareness programs for all employees on the prevention of sexual harassment, the provisions of the POSH Act, 2013, and the company's policy. This will ensure that all employees are aware of their rights, responsibilities, and the available mechanisms for reporting and redressal.

**13. Policy Review and Updates:**

This policy will be reviewed periodically and updated as necessary to ensure its effectiveness and compliance with any changes in legislation or organizational requirements. Acme Corp reserves the right to amend or modify this policy at any time.
