## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook

### **1. Introduction**

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a work environment that is both productive and supportive for all its employees. This Employee Handbook serves as a comprehensive guide to our company policies, procedures, and expectations. It is designed to ensure clarity, promote fairness, and uphold our commitment to ethical conduct and legal compliance.

All employees are expected to familiarize themselves with the contents of this handbook and adhere to the policies outlined herein. This handbook is a living document and may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legislation, company strategy, or operational needs.

Our mission is to empower businesses through cutting-edge technology and unparalleled expertise, driving growth and fostering innovation for our clients and our organization.

### **3. Employment Policies**

**3.1. Equal Employment Opportunity (EEO) Policy**

Acme Corp is an equal opportunity employer. We are committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment without regard to race, color, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, national origin, age, disability, genetic information, marital status, veteran status, or any other characteristic protected by applicable Indian laws. This policy applies to all terms and conditions of employment, including but not limited to hiring, placement, promotion, termination, layoff, recall, transfer, leaves of absence, compensation, and training.

**3.2. Anti-Discrimination and Anti-Harassment Policy**

Acme Corp strictly prohibits any form of unlawful discrimination or harassment in the workplace. This includes, but is not limited to, discrimination or harassment based on protected characteristics such as religion, caste, gender, disability, age, or any other basis prohibited by Indian law. We are committed to creating a work environment free from bullying, intimidation, and offensive behavior. Any employee found to have engaged in discriminatory or harassing behavior will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

**3.3. Recruitment and Selection Policy**

Acme Corp follows a fair and transparent recruitment and selection process. We aim to attract and hire qualified candidates based on merit, skills, and experience, ensuring compliance with all applicable labor laws in India. All hiring decisions will be made without regard to protected characteristics. We encourage internal applications for open positions and will consider qualified internal candidates before external recruitment.

**3.4. Employee Classification Policy**

Acme Corp classifies employees as either "exempt" or "non-exempt" in accordance with Indian labor laws. This classification determines eligibility for overtime pay and other benefits. Employees will be informed of their classification upon hiring.

**3.5. Employment Agreement Policy**

All employees will have an employment agreement outlining their role, responsibilities, compensation, benefits, and other terms of employment. Employees are expected to read and understand their employment agreement and adhere to its terms.

**3.6. Employee Code of Conduct Policy**

All employees are expected to adhere to a high standard of professional conduct. This includes:
*   **Compliance with Laws:** Adhering to all applicable Indian laws and regulations.
*   **Professionalism:** Maintaining a professional demeanor, appearance, and attitude.
*   **Respect for Others:** Treating colleagues, clients, and stakeholders with respect and dignity.
*   **Company Property:** Using company property responsibly and for business purposes only.
*   **Confidentiality:** Protecting sensitive company information.
*   **Conflict of Interest:** Avoiding situations where personal interests conflict with company interests.
*   **Honesty and Integrity:** Acting with honesty and integrity in all business dealings.

**3.7. Employee Probationary Period Policy**

New employees will undergo a probationary period of [Specify Duration, e.g., three months] to assess their suitability for the role and their fit within the company culture. During this period, performance will be closely monitored, and feedback will be provided. Successful completion of the probationary period is required for confirmation of employment.

**3.8. Employee Rehire Policy**

Former employees may be considered for rehire based on their previous performance, reason for departure, and the availability of suitable positions. Rehire decisions will be made on a case-by-case basis.

**3.9. Employee Termination Policy**

Acme Corp adheres to all legal requirements in India regarding employee terminations. Terminations may be voluntary (resignation) or involuntary (performance-related, misconduct, or redundancy). All terminations will be handled professionally and with due process.

**3.10. Employee Resignation Policy**

Employees intending to resign must provide written notice of at least [Specify Duration, e.g., two weeks or one month, as per contract/law] to their manager and HR. The company will conduct an exit interview to gather feedback.

**3.11. Layoff and Recall Procedures Policy**

In the event of workforce reductions, Acme Corp will follow fair and transparent layoff procedures in compliance with Indian labor laws. Affected employees may be eligible for severance pay and will be considered for recall if suitable positions become available.

**3.12. Whistleblower Policy**

Acme Corp encourages employees to report any suspected unethical, illegal, or improper conduct without fear of retaliation. The company has established channels for reporting such concerns and is committed to protecting whistleblowers.

**3.13. Workplace Violence Policy**

Acme Corp is committed to providing a safe work environment. Any form of workplace violence, threats, or aggressive behavior is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination.

**3.14. Workplace Harassment Policy**

Acme Corp has a zero-tolerance policy for workplace harassment, including sexual harassment. All employees are expected to treat each other with respect. Any incidents of harassment should be reported immediately to HR.

**3.15. Prevention of Sexual Harassment (POSH) Policy**

In accordance with the Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace (Prevention, Prohibition and Redressal) Act, 2013, Acme Corp has a comprehensive policy to prevent and address sexual harassment. An Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) has been established to handle complaints. All employees are expected to familiarize themselves with this policy.

**3.16. Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DEI) Policy**

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a diverse, equitable, and inclusive workplace where all employees feel valued, respected, and have equal opportunities to succeed. We celebrate diversity in all its forms and strive to create an environment where everyone can contribute their best.

**3.17. Drug Testing Policy**

Acme Corp maintains a drug-free workplace. The use, possession, or distribution of illegal drugs on company premises is strictly prohibited. Drug testing may be conducted under specific circumstances as outlined in the policy.

**3.18. Smoke-Free Workplace Policy**

Acme Corp maintains a smoke-free workplace. Smoking and the use of tobacco products are prohibited in all indoor and outdoor areas of the company premises.
