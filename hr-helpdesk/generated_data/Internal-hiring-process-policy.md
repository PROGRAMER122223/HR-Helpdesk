# Company Data Protection Policy

**1. Purpose**

This Company Data Protection Policy outlines Acme Corp's commitment to the lawful, fair, and transparent processing of personal data belonging to its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders. This policy ensures compliance with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA"), and upholds Acme Corp's dedication to safeguarding individual privacy rights.

**2. Scope**

This policy applies to all Acme Corp employees, contractors, consultants, and any third parties acting on behalf of Acme Corp who collect, process, store, or transmit personal data. It covers all personal data processed by Acme Corp, regardless of the format or medium in which it is held.

**3. Definitions**

*   **Personal Data:** Any data that relates to an identifiable individual, which can be directly or indirectly identified.
*   **Data Principal:** The individual to whom the personal data relates.
*   **Data Fiduciary:** The entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data (in this context, Acme Corp).
*   **Data Processor:** An entity that processes personal data on behalf of the Data Fiduciary.
*   **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, storage, adaptation, retrieval, use, disclosure, erasure, or destruction.

**4. Principles of Data Processing**

Acme Corp shall adhere to the following principles when processing personal data:

*   **Lawful and Fair Processing:** Personal data will be processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently, with the consent of the Data Principal where required by law.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will be collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a manner incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Data Minimization:** Only personal data that is adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary for the specified purposes will be collected and processed.
*   **Accuracy:** Personal data will be kept accurate and up-to-date. Inaccurate data will be rectified or erased without delay.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Personal data will be kept in an identifiable form for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Appropriate technical and organizational measures will be implemented to protect personal data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
*   **Accountability:** Acme Corp will be able to demonstrate compliance with these principles.

**5. Collection of Personal Data**

*   Personal data will only be collected with the explicit consent of the Data Principal, unless otherwise permitted by law.
*   Data Principals will be informed about the types of data being collected, the purpose of collection, and the intended use of their data.
*   Collection of sensitive personal data will be handled with extra care and in accordance with legal requirements.

**6. Processing of Personal Data**

*   Personal data will be processed only for the purposes for which it was collected.
*   Data Principals have the right to access, correct, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data, subject to legal provisions. Requests should be submitted to the designated contact person.
*   Acme Corp will ensure that Data Processors engaged by the company adhere to the same data protection standards.

**7. Data Security**

*   Acme Corp implements robust technical and organizational security measures to protect personal data. This includes, but is not limited to, access controls, encryption, regular security assessments, and secure data storage practices.
*   Access to personal data is granted on a need-to-know basis to authorized personnel only.

**8. Data Breach Notification**

*   In the event of a personal data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the nature and impact of the breach.
*   Acme Corp will notify the relevant regulatory authorities and affected Data Principals as required by the DPDPA and other applicable laws.

**9. Data Retention**

*   Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
*   Specific retention periods will be defined for different categories of personal data.

**10. Data Subject Rights**

Data Principals have the right to:

*   Access their personal data.
*   Request correction or erasure of their personal data.
*   Withdraw consent for the processing of their personal data.
*   Nominate another individual to exercise their rights in the event of death or incapacity.
*   Grievance Redressal: Data Principals can raise concerns or complaints regarding the processing of their personal data to the designated Data Protection Officer.

**11. Employee Training and Awareness**

*   All employees involved in processing personal data will receive regular training on data protection principles, security measures, and their responsibilities under this policy and relevant laws.
*   Awareness programs will be conducted to ensure all personnel understand the importance of data privacy.

**12. Compliance and Enforcement**

*   Adherence to this policy is mandatory for all individuals covered under its scope.
*   Any violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract, and may also lead to legal action as per applicable laws.
