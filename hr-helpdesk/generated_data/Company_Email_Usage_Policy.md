# Company Data Protection Policy

**Policy Version:** 1.0
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Review Date:** October 26, 2024

## 1. Policy Brief & Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, partners, and other stakeholders with the utmost care, confidentiality, and security. This Company Data Protection Policy outlines Acme Corp's commitment to fair, transparent, and lawful data processing practices, ensuring respect for individual rights in accordance with applicable data protection laws in India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023.

Our objective is to ensure that all personal data collected, processed, stored, and handled by Acme Corp is managed responsibly, safeguarding privacy and maintaining trust.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all individuals and entities who provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   All employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries.
*   Contractors, consultants, and third-party service providers acting on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   Job candidates and applicants.
*   Customers and clients.
*   Suppliers and vendors.
*   Any other party collaborating with or acting on behalf of Acme Corp.

## 3. Definitions

*   **Personal Data:** Any information relating to an identifiable natural person, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with Acme Corp, is capable of identifying such person. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial data, employment information, and online identifiers.
*   **Data Controller:** Acme Corp, as the entity that determines the purposes and means of processing personal data.
*   **Data Processor:** Any entity that processes personal data on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   **Processing:** Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure, or destruction.
*   **Consent:** Any freely given, specific, informed, and unambiguous indication of the data principal's wishes by which they signify their agreement to the processing of personal data relating to them.

## 4. Principles of Data Protection

Acme Corp adheres to the following core principles when processing personal data:

*   **Lawfulness, Fairness, and Transparency:** Personal data will be processed lawfully, fairly, and in a transparent manner. Data subjects will be informed about data collection and processing activities.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will be collected for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes and not further processed in a manner that is incompatible with those purposes.
*   **Data Minimization:** Personal data collected will be adequate, relevant, and limited to what is necessary in relation to the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Accuracy:** Personal data will be accurate and, where necessary, kept up to date. Every reasonable step will be taken to ensure that inaccurate personal data is erased or rectified without delay.
*   **Storage Limitation:** Personal data will be kept in a form that permits identification of data subjects for no longer than is necessary for the purposes for which the personal data is processed.
*   **Integrity and Confidentiality:** Personal data will be processed in a manner that ensures appropriate security, including protection against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage, using appropriate technical or organizational measures.
*   **Accountability:** Acme Corp will be responsible for, and be able to demonstrate compliance with, the principles relating to the processing of personal data.

## 5. Collection of Personal Data

Acme Corp collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate business purposes. This data is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed. Personal data may be collected through various channels, including but not limited to:

*   Online forms and website interactions.
*   Emails and direct communications.
*   In-person interactions and applications.
*   Employment applications and HR processes.
*   Customer service interactions.

Data subjects will be informed about the purpose of data collection at the point of collection.

## 6. Processing of Personal Data

Acme Corp processes personal data in a fair, transparent, and lawful manner, strictly for the purposes for which it was collected. We will obtain necessary consents where required by law.

Data subjects have the right to:

*   Access their personal data.
*   Rectify or correct inaccurate personal data.
*   Erase their personal data (subject to legal obligations).
*   Restrict or object to the processing of their personal data.
*   Port their personal data to another data controller.

Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated Data Protection Officer.

## 7. Data Security

Acme Corp implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data. This includes:

*   Restricting access to personal data to employees and authorized third parties on a need-to-know basis.
*   Implementing physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
*   Regularly reviewing and updating security measures to address evolving threats.
*   Ensuring that third-party processors also maintain adequate security measures.

## 8. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the impact and, if required by law, notify the relevant Data Protection Board and affected data subjects without undue delay. The notification will include details of the breach, its potential consequences, and the measures taken or proposed to be taken by Acme Corp.

## 9. Data Transfer

Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when adequate safeguards are in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with applicable data protection laws. Such transfers will only occur with explicit consent or where legally permissible.

## 10. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Acme Corp has established specific retention periods for different types of data, and data will be securely disposed of or anonymized once it is no longer needed.

## 11. Data Subject Rights

Acme Corp respects the rights of data subjects concerning their personal data. These rights include:

*   Right to access personal data.
*   Right to correction and erasure of personal data.
*   Right to grievance redressal.
*   Right to nominate another person to exercise rights in the event of death or incapacity.

## 13. Employee Responsibilities

All employees who handle personal data are responsible for:

*   Understanding and complying with this policy.
*   Completing mandatory data protection training.
*   Reporting any suspected data breaches or policy violations immediately.
*   Handling personal data with care and confidentiality.

## 14. Training and Awareness

Acme Corp will provide regular training to employees on data protection principles, policies, and procedures to ensure awareness and understanding.

## 15. Policy Review and Updates

This policy will be reviewed periodically, at least annually, to reflect changes in data processing activities, legal requirements, or business operations. Any updates or amendments to this policy will be communicated to all relevant parties.

## 16. Compliance and Consequences

Adherence to this policy is mandatory for all individuals covered under its scope. Non-compliance with this policy, including any breach of data protection guidelines, will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, and may also lead to legal action as per applicable laws.
