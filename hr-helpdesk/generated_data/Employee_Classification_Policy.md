## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Data Privacy Act India Compliance

**Policy Number:** ACME-HR-DP-001
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose and Brief

Acme Corp is committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and confidentiality for all individuals whose personal data we collect, process, store, or handle. This policy outlines Acme Corp's commitment to complying with the applicable data protection laws in India, including but not limited to the Personal Data Protection Bill (when enacted) ("Data Protection Laws"). Our aim is to ensure that personal data is managed fairly, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all parties who provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   All employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries.
*   Job candidates and applicants.
*   Customers and clients.
*   Suppliers and vendors.
*   Contractors, consultants, and any other external entities collaborating with or acting on behalf of Acme Corp.

### 3. Definitions

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to a natural person, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with Acme Corp, is capable of identifying such person.
*   **Data Controller:** Acme Corp, as the entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.
*   **Data Processor:** Any entity that processes personal data on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   **Processing:** Any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.

### 4. Collection of Personal Data

*   **Lawful Purpose:** Acme Corp collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. The data collected is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
*   **Transparency:** We will inform individuals about the types of personal data collected, the purposes of processing, and the rights they have regarding their data.
*   **Consent:** Where required by Data Protection Laws, Acme Corp will obtain explicit consent for the collection and processing of personal data.

### 5. Processing of Personal Data

*   **Fairness and Lawfulness:** Acme Corp processes personal data in a fair, transparent, and lawful manner. Personal data is processed only for the purposes for which it was collected.
*   **Data Minimization:** We will only collect and process personal data that is adequate, relevant, and necessary for the specified purposes.
*   **Purpose Limitation:** Personal data will not be processed in a manner that is incompatible with the purposes for which it was originally collected.
*   **Individual Rights:** Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data. Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated Data Protection Officer.

### 6. Data Security

*   **Technical and Organizational Measures:** Acme Corp implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data. This includes measures to protect against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
*   **Access Control:** Access to personal data is restricted to employees and authorized third parties on a need-to-know basis, based on their roles and responsibilities.

### 7. Data Breach Notification

*   **Prompt Assessment:** In the event of a data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the impact and, if required by Data Protection Laws, notify the relevant data protection authorities and affected individuals without undue delay.

### 8. Data Transfer

*   **Adequate Safeguards:** Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when there are adequate safeguards in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the Data Protection Laws.

### 9. Data Retention

*   **Necessity and Law:** Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Acme Corp has established specific retention periods for different types of data.

### 10. Employee Obligations

*   **Compliance:** All employees are required to comply with this Policy and attend regular training on data privacy matters as provided by Acme Corp.
*   **Reporting:** Employees must report any suspected data breaches or violations of this policy immediately to the designated Data Protection Officer.

### 11. Updates to the Policy

*   **Periodic Review:** This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in Acme Corp's data processing activities or changes in the Data Protection Laws. Employees will be notified of any updates to this Policy.
