## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Remote Work

**Policy Number:** HR-REM-001
**Effective Date:** October 26, 2023
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to fostering a flexible and productive work environment that supports employee well-being and operational efficiency. This Remote Work Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for employees who are approved to work from a location other than a designated Acme Corp office. The policy aims to ensure continued productivity, maintain data security, and uphold company standards while offering flexibility to eligible employees.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all full-time and part-time employees of Acme Corp who are approved for remote work arrangements. This policy does not apply to employees who are required to work on-site due to the nature of their role or who are engaged on a temporary or contract basis, unless specifically stated in their contract.

### 3. Eligibility for Remote Work

3.1. **Role Suitability:** Eligibility for remote work is primarily determined by the nature of the employee's role and responsibilities. Roles that can be performed effectively with minimal need for physical presence at an Acme Corp office, and that do not require access to specialized on-site equipment or sensitive physical data, are generally considered eligible.

3.2. **Performance Standards:** Employees must consistently meet or exceed performance expectations and demonstrate a strong track record of reliability, self-discipline, and effective communication.

3.3. **Managerial Approval:** All remote work arrangements require the explicit approval of the employee's direct manager and may require further approval from department heads or HR, depending on the circumstances.

3.4. **Infrastructure:** Employees must have a dedicated, safe, and ergonomic workspace at their remote location, equipped with a reliable internet connection that meets Acme Corp's technical requirements.

### 4. Types of Remote Work Arrangements

Acme Corp recognizes various remote work models:

4.1. **Fully Remote:** Employees work exclusively from a location outside of an Acme Corp office.

4.2. **Hybrid:** Employees split their work time between an Acme Corp office and an approved remote location. Specific schedules (e.g., days in office vs. days remote) will be agreed upon by the employee and their manager.

4.3. **Remote Permitted:** The primary work location is an Acme Corp office, but occasional remote work may be permitted with manager approval for specific circumstances.

### 5. Responsibilities

5.1. **Employee Responsibilities:**
    *   Maintain productivity and meet all job performance standards.
    *   Adhere to agreed-upon work hours and be available during core business hours as defined by their manager.
    *   Ensure a secure and distraction-free remote work environment.
    *   Maintain the confidentiality and security of all Acme Corp data and information.
    *   Utilize company-provided or approved technology and communication tools.
    *   Communicate proactively with managers and team members regarding work status, availability, and any challenges.
    *   Comply with all Acme Corp policies and procedures, including those related to data security, IT usage, and code of conduct.
    *   Report any changes in remote work location or circumstances that may affect eligibility or security.

5.2. **Manager Responsibilities:**
    *   Assess role suitability for remote work and approve/deny requests based on policy guidelines and business needs.
    *   Clearly define performance expectations, communication protocols, and availability requirements for remote employees.
    *   Ensure equitable distribution of work and opportunities among remote and on-site team members.
    *   Conduct regular check-ins and performance discussions with remote employees.
    *   Address any concerns related to remote work productivity, security, or well-being.
    *   Ensure compliance with this policy within their team.

5.3. **IT Department Responsibilities:**
    *   Provide and support necessary technology and secure access to company systems.
    *   Establish and enforce security protocols for remote work.
    *   Assist employees with technical issues related to remote work setups.

5.4. **Human Resources Department Responsibilities:**
    *   Administer and interpret this policy.
    *   Provide guidance and support to employees and managers regarding remote work arrangements.
    *   Ensure compliance with all applicable labor laws and regulations.
    *   Maintain records of approved remote work arrangements.

### 6. Work Hours and Availability

6.1. **Core Hours:** Employees working remotely are expected to be available and responsive during Acme Corp's core business hours, typically [Specify Core Hours, e.g., 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST], unless otherwise agreed upon with their manager.

6.2. **Flexibility:** While flexibility is a key benefit of remote work, employees must ensure their work schedule allows for effective collaboration and timely completion of tasks. Any deviations from standard work hours must be approved by the manager.

6.3. **Time Tracking:** All employees, regardless of work location, are required to accurately record their working hours using Acme Corp's designated timekeeping system.

### 7. Data Security and Confidentiality

7.1. **Secure Environment:** Remote employees must ensure their remote workspace and any devices used for work are secure. This includes using strong passwords, enabling device encryption, and avoiding public Wi-Fi for accessing sensitive company data.

7.2. **Confidential Information:** All Acme Corp confidential information, including client data, proprietary information, and intellectual property, must be protected at all times. Employees must adhere to Acme Corp's Data Protection and Information Security policies.

7.3. **Device Usage:** Company-issued equipment must be used primarily for business purposes. Personal use should be minimal and must not interfere with work responsibilities or compromise security.

7.4. **Reporting Breaches:** Any suspected or actual data breaches, security incidents, or loss of company equipment must be reported immediately to the IT Department and HR.

### 8. Equipment and Resources

8.1. **Company-Provided Equipment:** Acme Corp will provide necessary equipment such as laptops, monitors, and software licenses to eligible remote employees. Employees are responsible for the care and maintenance of company-provided equipment.

8.2. **Employee-Provided Resources:** Employees are responsible for ensuring they have a reliable internet connection and a suitable workspace. Acme Corp may provide a stipend to contribute towards internet or home office expenses, as outlined in the Employee Benefits policy.

8.3. **Technical Support:** Remote employees can access IT support through [Specify IT Support Channels, e.g., helpdesk portal, designated email, phone number].

### 9. Performance Management

9.1. **Performance Expectations:** Performance standards and expectations remain the same for remote employees as for on-site employees. Performance will be evaluated based on results, productivity, and adherence to job responsibilities.

9.2. **Feedback and Reviews:** Managers will conduct regular performance reviews and provide ongoing feedback to remote employees, utilizing virtual communication tools as needed.

### 10. Expenses and Reimbursement

10.1. **Reimbursable Expenses:** Acme Corp will reimburse employees for pre-approved, business-related expenses incurred while working remotely, in accordance with the Company Expense Policy. This may include internet service, phone usage, and necessary office supplies.

10.2. **Approval:** All expense reimbursements must be pre-approved by the employee's manager and submitted with appropriate documentation, following the procedures outlined in the Company Expense Policy.

### 11. Termination of Remote Work Arrangement

11.1. **Company Discretion:** Acme Corp reserves the right to modify, suspend, or terminate an employee's remote work arrangement at any time, with or without notice, based on business needs, performance issues, policy violations, or changes in role requirements.

11.2. **Employee Request:** Employees may request to end their remote work arrangement and return to an on-site role. Such requests will be considered based on available positions and business needs.

11.3. **Return of Property:** Upon termination of employment or the remote work arrangement, employees must return all company-owned equipment and property in good working condition, as per the Company Assets Policy.

### 12. Compliance and Policy Violations

12.1. **Adherence:** All employees approved for remote work must adhere to this policy and all other applicable Acme Corp policies.

12.2. **Consequences:** Failure to comply with this Remote Work Policy may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment, and revocation of remote work privileges.

### 13. Policy Review

This policy will be reviewed annually by the Human Resources department and may be updated as necessary to reflect changes in business needs, technology, or legal requirements. Employees will be notified of any significant changes.
