# Company Data Protection Policy

## 1. Policy Brief & Purpose

Acme Corp is committed to treating the personal data of its employees, customers, stakeholders, and other interested parties with the utmost care, confidentiality, and in compliance with all applicable data protection laws in India, including the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 ("DPDPA"). This policy ensures that Acme Corp gathers, stores, and handles data fairly, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to all individuals and entities that provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp. This includes:

*   **Employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries.**
*   **Contractors, consultants, partners, and any other external entities collaborating with or acting on behalf of Acme Corp.**
*   **Job candidates, customers, suppliers, and any other interested parties who share their personal data with Acme Corp.**

## 3. Policy Elements

As part of its operations, Acme Corp collects and processes personal data. This data includes any offline or online information that makes a person identifiable, such as names, addresses, contact details, identification numbers, financial data, digital footprints, and other relevant personal information.

Acme Corp collects this information transparently and only with the explicit consent of the data principal (the individual to whom the data pertains) for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. Once this information is available to Acme Corp, the following rules apply:

### 3.1. Data Handling Principles

Acme Corp's data will be:

*   **Accurate and Kept Up-to-Date:** Reasonable steps will be taken to ensure data is accurate and updated as necessary.
*   **Collected Fairly and for Lawful Purposes Only:** Data will be collected only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes as defined by the DPDPA.
*   **Processed Lawfully and Ethically:** Personal data will be processed in accordance with the DPDPA and Acme Corp's ethical standards.
*   **Protected Against Unauthorized Access:** Robust technical and organizational measures will be implemented to protect data against unauthorized or unlawful processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage.

Acme Corp's data will not be:

*   **Communicated Informally:** Data sharing will adhere to strict protocols and only be communicated to authorized parties.
*   **Stored for Longer Than Necessary:** Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
*   **Transferred to Entities Without Adequate Protection:** Personal data will not be transferred to organizations, states, or countries that do not have adequate data protection policies, unless specific safeguards are in place as per the DPDPA.
*   **Distributed to Unauthorized Parties:** Data will not be distributed to any party other than those agreed upon by the data principal, except for legitimate requests from law enforcement authorities or as required by law.

### 3.2. Obligations Towards Data Principals

Acme Corp has direct obligations towards individuals whose data it holds. Specifically, Acme Corp must:

*   **Inform Data Principals:** Clearly inform individuals about which of their personal data is collected.
*   **Explain Data Processing:** Inform individuals about how their data will be processed and for what purposes.
*   **Disclose Data Access:** Inform individuals about who has access to their information.
*   **Manage Data Breaches:** Have provisions in place for handling cases of lost, corrupted, or compromised data.
*   **Facilitate Data Rights:** Allow individuals to request that Acme Corp modify, erase, restrict the processing of, or correct data contained in its databases.

## 4. Actions to Exercise Data Protection

Acme Corp is committed to implementing robust data protection measures, including:

*   **Restricting and Monitoring Access:** Implementing access controls and monitoring systems to limit and track access to sensitive personal data.
*   **Developing Transparent Data Collection Procedures:** Ensuring data collection methods are clear and understandable to data principals.
*   **Training Employees:** Providing regular training to employees on online privacy, data security measures, and the DPDPA.
*   **Building Secure Networks:** Establishing and maintaining secure networks and systems to protect online data from cyberattacks.
*   **Establishing Breach Reporting Procedures:** Implementing clear procedures for reporting privacy breaches or data misuse.
*   **Contractual Clauses and Communications:** Including data protection clauses in contracts with third parties and communicating data handling practices clearly.
*   **Implementing Data Protection Practices:** Establishing physical and digital data protection practices, such as secure document shredding, locked storage, data encryption, frequent backups, and access authorization protocols.
*   **Website Transparency:** Ensuring data protection provisions are clearly stated on Acme Corp's website.

## 5. Disciplinary Consequences

All principles described in this policy must be strictly followed. A breach of data protection guidelines will invoke disciplinary and possibly legal action, in accordance with the DPDPA and other applicable Indian laws. Consequences may include, but are not limited to, warnings, suspension, termination of employment, and legal penalties.
