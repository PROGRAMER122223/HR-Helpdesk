## Acme Corp - Employee Handbook Policy: Data Privacy Act India Compliance

**Policy Number:** ACME-HR-DP-001
**Effective Date:** 2023-10-27
**Version:** 1.0

---

### 1. Purpose and Brief

Acme Corp is committed to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and protection for all individuals whose personal data we collect, process, store, or handle. This policy outlines our commitment to complying with the applicable data protection laws in India, including but not limited to the Personal Data Protection Bill, when enacted ("Data Protection Laws"). Our aim is to ensure that personal data is managed fairly, transparently, and with respect for individual rights.

### 2. Scope

This policy applies to all parties who provide any amount of personal data to Acme Corp. This includes, but is not limited to:

*   Employees of Acme Corp and its subsidiaries.
*   Job candidates.
*   Customers.
*   Suppliers and vendors.
*   Contractors, consultants, and any other external entities acting on behalf of Acme Corp or collaborating with us.

All individuals covered by this policy are expected to adhere to its principles and procedures.

### 3. Definitions

*   **Personal Data:** Any information that relates to a natural person, which either directly or indirectly, in combination with other information available or likely to be available with Acme Corp, is capable of identifying such person.
*   **Data Controller:** Acme Corp, as the entity that determines the purposes and means of the processing of personal data.
*   **Data Processor:** Any entity that processes personal data on behalf of Acme Corp.
*   **Processing:** Any operation or set of operations performed on personal data, whether or not by automated means, such as collection, recording, organization, structuring, storage, adaptation or alteration, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination or otherwise making available, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure or destruction.

### 4. Collection of Personal Data

4.1. **Purpose Limitation:** Acme Corp collects personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes. The data collected is relevant and limited to what is necessary for the purposes for which it is processed.
4.2. **Transparency:** We will inform individuals about the types of personal data collected, the purposes of processing, and the rights they have regarding their data.
4.3. **Consent:** Where required by Data Protection Laws, we will obtain explicit consent for the collection and processing of personal data.
4.4. **Channels:** Personal data may be collected through various channels, including but not limited to online forms, website interactions, emails, in-person interactions, and employment applications.

### 5. Processing of Personal Data

5.1. **Fairness and Lawfulness:** Acme Corp processes personal data in a fair, transparent, and lawful manner. Personal data is processed only for the purposes for which it was collected.
5.2. **Data Minimization:** We will only collect and process personal data that is adequate, relevant, and necessary for the specified purposes.
5.3. **Accuracy:** We will take reasonable steps to ensure that personal data is accurate and kept up-to-date.
5.4. **Individual Rights:** Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict the processing of their personal data. Requests to exercise these rights can be made to the designated Data Protection Officer.

### 6. Data Security

6.1. **Technical and Organizational Measures:** Acme Corp implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure the security and confidentiality of personal data. This includes measures to protect against unauthorized or unlawful processing and against accidental loss, destruction, or damage.
6.2. **Access Control:** Access to personal data is restricted to employees and authorized third parties on a need-to-know basis. Access is granted based on job roles and responsibilities.
6.3. **Regular Audits:** Security measures and data handling practices will be subject to regular audits to ensure ongoing effectiveness and compliance.

### 7. Data Breach Notification

7.1. **Prompt Assessment:** In the event of a data breach, Acme Corp will promptly assess the impact and potential risks to individuals.
7.2. **Notification:** If required by Data Protection Laws, we will notify the relevant data protection authorities and affected individuals without undue delay. Notification will include details of the breach, its likely consequences, and the measures taken or proposed to be taken.

### 8. Data Transfer

8.1. **Adequate Safeguards:** Personal data may be transferred to third parties or international entities only when there are adequate safeguards in place to ensure the protection of the data and compliance with the Data Protection Laws.
8.2. **Third-Party Agreements:** We will ensure that any third-party processors acting on our behalf adhere to data protection principles and contractual obligations equivalent to those outlined in this policy.

### 9. Data Retention

9.1. **Necessity:** Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.
9.2. **Retention Periods:** Acme Corp has established specific retention periods for different types of data, which are documented and regularly reviewed. Data will be securely disposed of or anonymized once the retention period expires.

### 10. Updates to the Policy

10.1. **Periodic Review:** This Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in Acme Corp's data processing activities, business practices, or changes in the Data Protection Laws.
10.2. **Notification:** Employees will be notified of any updates to this Policy through appropriate communication channels, such as company-wide emails or updates to the employee handbook.

### 11. Compliance and Training

11.1. **Employee Obligation:** All employees are required to comply with this Policy and attend regular training sessions on data privacy matters.
11.2. **Awareness:** Employees must be aware of their responsibilities regarding the handling of personal data and the importance of data protection.
